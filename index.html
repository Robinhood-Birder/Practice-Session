Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 Minutes

  • Preparation: 5 Minutes

  • Cooking: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large Eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 2 table-spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Cook the eggs in a pan over medium heat until they are cooked through and slightly set.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the cooked eggs to the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until they are just set.
  4. Add the mashed potatoes and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender.
  5. Add the flour, salt, and pepper to the pan and stir until combined.
